About us

Welcome to Tails&Wiggles family

where compassionate hearts and furry friends find their forever homes. Our mission is to unite loving families with pets in need, creating lifelong bonds of joy and companionship.We believe in the transformative power of pet adoption. Every wagging tail, gentle purr, and joyful bark has the potential to bring immeasurable happiness to both the pet and their new family. We strive to make the adoption process a seamless and joyful experience, connecting those seeking companionship with the perfect furry match.

Why us?

What sets us apart is our commitment to responsible pet ownership. We provide valuable resources and support to adopters, guiding them through the transition period and offering advice on everything from training tips to veterinary care.
